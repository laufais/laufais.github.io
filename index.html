Welcome to public.me.

Create wishlist, habit tracker, list of places to visit and tasks to fix.
All in Apple Reminders.
And then share it with your friends, by publishing online.

The steps are simple:

  1. Go to any Apple Reminders list you want to publish online.
  2. Connect it to public.me.
  3. Make any changes to your note and wait...
  4. ... everything will be published to the web automatically.
